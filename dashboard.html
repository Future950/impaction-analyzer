Classification Guide
Reference for Pell & Gregory and Winter's classification systems used in this analyzer
Pell & Gregory
Relates wisdom tooth position to the ramus of mandible (class) and occlusal plane (depth). Primary system for mandibular 3rd molars.
Class — Ramus Relationship
I
Adequate space
Crown fully anterior to the anterior ramus border — sufficient space for eruption
II
Partial coverage
Part of the crown covered by the ramus — less than full mesiodistal diameter available
III
Full inclusion
Tooth completely embedded within the ramus — no space for eruption
Depth — Occlusal Plane
A
Coronal level
Highest point of crown at or above the occlusal plane of the 2nd molar
B
Mid level
Crown between occlusal plane and cervical line of the 2nd molar
C
Apical level
Crown below the cervical line of the 2nd molar — deeply impacted
Winter's Classification
Describes the angulation of the long axis of the impacted tooth relative to the long axis of the adjacent 2nd molar.
MA
Mesioangular
Crown tilted toward the 2nd molar (~30–80°). Most common type. Higher risk of 2nd molar root resorption.
DA
Distoangular
Crown tilted away from 2nd molar (~-30 to -80°). Most difficult surgical access.
V
Vertical
Crown parallel to 2nd molar (<30°). Second most common. Moderate extraction difficulty.
H
Horizontal
Long axis perpendicular to 2nd molar (>80°). Crown pointing directly at root. Complex extraction.
T
Transverse
Tooth tilted bucco-lingually. Crown not visible in panoramic — CBCT required for full assessment.
IN
Inverted
Crown facing apically (upside-down). Rarest form. Often associated with cyst formation.
Process DICOM Files
Run the analyzer on your Carestream DICOM exports and populate the database
Command line usage
# Process a folder of DICOM files
python impaction_analyzer.py /path/to/dicoms/ --db dental_impactions.db

# Process specific files
python impaction_analyzer.py patient1.dcm patient2.dcm --db dental_impactions.db

# Also export JSON
python impaction_analyzer.py /dicoms/ --db dental_impactions.db --export-json results.json

# View database summary only
python impaction_analyzer.py --summary --db dental_impactions.db
